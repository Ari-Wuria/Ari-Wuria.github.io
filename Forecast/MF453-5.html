Friday Special Session: The (delayed) Monday Presentations!
 
 
 

30 May 2025, 18:30

HXLY 213 Clore LT

 

Presenation Monday replacement session

 

For those who are not aware, we are trailing a new Monday event: The presentation Monday. This is a chance to present/share any ACGN(anime, manga, games, light novels)-related topic of your choice! You could for example:  

 

-share & recommend an anime/manga/light novel/game that you really liked

 

-do a in-depth literary analysis of your favourite anime and/or character

 

-make a lecture (and add in mentimetre quizzes...?). 

 

-roast bad anime that you absolutely hate

 

-make meme analysis of anime

 

- tell funny jokes...

 

so long it's suitable.

 

Currently we're allocating two time slots per session to allow two presenters to share their ideas. Please sign up through the button below

 

Put in your name (or nicknames but make sure we can know who you are), and your proposed presentation title in the time slot you wish to present.

 

Each presenter could present up to 40 minutes per time slot - after that there would be 20 minutes left for any questions from the audience.

ICON
 
 
 

31 May 2025, 11:00-19:00

Metric

 

This event is not hosted by ICAS and the information are supplied by the event organisers. Please contact them directly for any enquiries or questions. 

 

The first wave of tickets is live now for only £3.75 and will be available until the 21st of May 6PM on the Imperial College Pokemon Society Union Page! This is the cheapest the tickets will be so get them soon! Further tickets will be available but at a higher price!

 

ICon is Imperials’ nerdiest convention! Enjoy the activity stalls run by societies, watch live performances by talented students or shop from independent student businesses!

 

When buying, note:

• Any uni student can attend!

• Use a valid, uni email when buying

• Bring a valid, physical, uni ID when attending

• No ticket is needed to buy from sellers! Just show up, and shop 'til you drop!

• If you purchase multiple tickets everyone must attend with the purchaser and everyone must bring a valid uni ID

• If identification is not brought you will not be allowed to enter the venue and will not be refunded

 

WHAT'S ON

ICon has many fun activities, for both the hardcore nerd and the casual fan, including:

• Society activities and stalls: videogame stations, robotic shenanigans, and more!

• A look at student-led projects, indie student game developers and student music artists!

• Performances by bands, a K-Pop random play, and a mini-VOCALOID concert!

• Independent student businesses and societies, who you can buy form without a ticket!

• What to know what else? Buy a ticket and come along to find out (or follow our socials, where we'll be promoting every activity in the lead up to the event)!
