Leadership Election 2025

Last week of election nomination! Thanks to all of the nominations, we currently have the following positions empty: 

Ambassador

Artist-in-Residence

 

If you’re passionate about supporting ICAS, this is a great opportunity to join the team for next year, make a difference, and help us continue to grow!

 

The election nomination is live, and you can stand for these positions via the eVoting website until 12:00, 28 Feb 2025. You must have full membership of ICAS to be qualified as candidate in addition to standard eligibility criteria. You can access the voting site by pressing on the button below. More information regarding the election in general is available on the Union's Website.

 

You will be able to see the duties of all the committee roles in our constitution on our website.

 

To stand for a position, simply submit a manifesto and a photo (if you'd like to, there's an option to submit nothing too) through the eVoting platform to introduce yourself to the members. After the nomination period ends, we will open election channels in our Discord where members can ask questions to the candidates.
 
 

Nominate yourself here!
