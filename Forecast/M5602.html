Hi fellow ICAS members!

 

Welcome to the new academic year, and to our new members, welcome to Imperial College AnimeSoc! We want to provide some important dates and information about the first week. We have a jam-packed first week ahead for you!

 

This year marks the 25th Anniversary of Imperial College AnimeSoc. We focus on promoting anime, manga, games, and related J-culture, while providing a space for members to socialise and connect.

 

To kick off this year, we have an exciting lineup of events planned for you. Here’s a sneak peek at what’s coming up:

 

 - Welcome Fair

 - Welcome Event

 - LAU Halloween Event @ KCL

 - MCM

 - Melon Band Winter Live

 - And more!

 
 
 
 

Before we go into details....

Join our Discord to participate in our online discussions! Although the mailing list is the only official announcment source, sneek peek will go into our Discord. We are also a very online-active community (of course) so join and socialise along with other members!

 

We also have other social media account such as our Instagram and facebook. Please make sure to follow them too!

 

Please also support us by purchasing our membership. Although you are more than welcome to join our first welcome session without a membership, purchasing it will allow you to access our bigger events, obtaining band live ticket discounts, and much more!

Our first official event will be the introductory session at 18:30 Friday, 10th October, in Huxley 130. We will introduce the club, have a quiz, and have some fun! Please save the date and join us!
 
 
 
 

Melon Band Winter Live

For those of you who just joined ICAS, our very own Melon Band performs one live each semester! Currently, the Winter Live for this semester is provisionally on 22th November weekend. We are awaiting final approval from the Union and will announce it officially once we heard back! 

 

As a tradition, ICAS will get to vote for an anime or anime film for its song to be included in the Winter Live. The Band will perform a song from the most voted anime in the next concert!

 

This must be from the Spring 2025 or Summer 2025 seasons, or else the response won't be counted. You can find detailed eligibility creteria in the voting form. 

 

The deadline for voting is 23:59, 12 Oct 2025 BST. 
 
 

Vote for your favourite!
