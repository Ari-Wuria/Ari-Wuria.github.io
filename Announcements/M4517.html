Gunpla Building Event
 
 
 

Wed 19th Feb, 14:00 RSM G.20

 

Hey guys ICAS will be hosting a Gunpla building (assembling plastic model kits of mechs from the Gundam series) session next Wednesday (19th Feb)!
 
We'll be providing 10 members with Gunpla Kits under ￡25 - a great chance for those who wants to give it a shot!
 
If you would be interested in a FREE gunpla kit please make sure to register your interest here, in the form below. First come first served!
 
If you're requesting for gunpla kits - you could choose your desired Gunpla kit from this catalogue (we'll be buying from that store!)
 
 
You could list up to 3 Gunplas (ideally HG kits in 2) that you would like and we'll try to get one for you; alternatively you could also type in 'any' and let us do the magic.
 
We'll also be providing some essential tools for Gunpla building - also make sure to register your interest in this form! Note that membership is required for the free Gunpla kits.
 
If you would be interested, please make sure to purchase our membership before filling in this form :) See you there!
